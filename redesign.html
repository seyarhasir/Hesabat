🗂️ Navigation Structure

FAB + Button

Opens New Sale instantly

The most frequent action. One tap from anywhere in the app — no going back to Home first.

Qarz Tab

Debtors only, not all customers

Only shows people who owe money. Clean, focused list with Pay button visible immediately.

More → Customers

Full customer list lives here

All customers including those with no debt. Separated into "People" section at top of More screen so it's easy to find but not cluttering daily flow.

More → Sections

Grouped by purpose

People → Customers
Operations → Inventory, Reports
App → Settings, Subscription
